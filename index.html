how to play

Use the keys 'A', 'S', 'D', & 'F' to send a line of that color upwards towards the falling lines. Match the colors and don't let them reach the bottom!

Change the speed of the falling lines by pressing the change level button. But you must change the level before the start of the game. Give it a try!
